Arvind LED: Adhesive LED Screens - Transform Any Glass Surface into a Dynamic Display

Revolutionize your visual communication with Arvind LED's innovative Adhesive LED Screens. These ultra-thin, transparent displays adhere seamlessly to glass and other smooth surfaces, turning ordinary windows into captivating digital canvases.

Why Choose Arvind LED Adhesive Solutions?

  • 5mm Screen Thickness and 10kg each square meter.
  • 14-16 bit Grayscale Rating restores every subtle color and display the ultimate exquisite picture quality.
  • Transparency up to 85%, without visual impact on the original building
  • Customized the panel sizes according to the project requirements freely
  • Frameless design, can quickly and seamlessly splice and stick any screen size on the glass
