Arvind LED: Indoor Transparent LED Screens - Blend Digital Display with Architectural Design

Elevate your indoor spaces with Arvind LED's Indoor Transparent LED Screens. These innovative displays seamlessly integrate digital content with your environment, offering a stunning visual experience without obstructing views or natural light.

Why Choose Arvind LED Indoor Transparent Solutions?

  • Ultra-thin transparent screen box, the thickness is only 80 mm, and the thinnest part of the edge is only 20 mm.
  • Good heat dissipation for energy saving and environmental protection,
  • Transparency can reach more than 759%, without affecting the indoor lighting
  • Pluggable and Modular design, easy to connection operation and save maintenance costs.
  • High & low brightness solutions available. 5000 nit highlight could be visible clearly under strong light.
  • Hang installation, bottom bracket type or ceiling type installation.
