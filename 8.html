Arvind LED: Curtain LED Screens - Lightweight, Flexible, and Dynamically Engaging.

Transform any space into a captivating visual spectacle with Arvind LED's Curtain LED Screens. These lightweight and flexible displays offer a unique blend of transparency and dynamic visual capabilities, perfect for creating stunning stage backdrops, architectural features, and immersive environments.

Why Choose Arvind Curtain LED Screen?

  • 50% transparency, making the building curtain wall into a realistic video wall.
  • Light weight design, 18kg/m², easy to install and maintain.
  • When installed on the wall of a building, no steel structure is required
  • Using high-brightness LED and high conversion efficiency power supply, the power consumption is more than 50% less than that of traditional products.
  • For the first time in the industry, the entire screen has an IP67 protection level, effectively extending the service life of the product
  • Works trouble-free at temperatures-30 C to 60 C", and the wind-resistant design can withstand strong winds.
